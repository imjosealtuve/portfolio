Pellizco

This is a game where each player must connect the points in ascending order, i.e., from 1 to 2, from 2 to 3, until reaching number 10. The difficulty of the game lies in not touching the other drawn lines; if the player does, a point is added to their score. Additionally, the player cannot release the click, or they will lose their turn. The player with the fewest points wins.
